What is Geo-Leasing?

Geo-Leasing is a brand new way for premium domains to become more accessible for everyday businesses without AAA marketing budgets - at no risk to the premium domain owners.

We all know that you can exclusively buy or lease a domain, DynaDomains Geo-Lease Technology allows someone to draw a custom polygon on a map, where anyone physically inside that polygon will be redirected to another website when visiting the premium domain being leased. This all happens in under a second and for a very low monthly rate. When you draw a polygon, you only pay for the geography you want. You own that geographic area exclusively!

On-board a premium (or unused) domain you have with DynaDomains and make monthly income while still being able to sell or traditionally lease it. Setup with a simple 301 redirect and you're done!
